I am a PhD student in Computer Science at [Portland State University](http://www.pdx.edu). I bring a diverse educational background, having earned two master's degrees in Artificial Intelligence from [Iran University of Science and Technology](http://www.iust.ac.ir/en) and Technology Management from the [University of Tehran](https://ut.ac.ir/en). My undergraduate degree was in Industrial Engineering.

My passion lies in the field of artificial intelligence and innovation. I have hands-on experience in software engineering and a proven track record in developing effective technology development strategies. My research interests include Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence.
