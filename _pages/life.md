Music is one of my hobbies. I can play Tar, Setar and Oud (Persian musical instruments) and I learnt them from famous Iranian artists such as 
Mohammad Reza Lotfi, Masoud Shaari, Arshad Tahmasebi and Mohammad Reza Ebrahimi. Nowadays, I record some music with my friends in our small music studio every week and share them in our instagram and youtube. It's fun and I love it.
