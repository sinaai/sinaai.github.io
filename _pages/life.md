I live with my love Setareh. We got married since Sep 2014 and we live in Karaj, Iran. She is a software engineer and a graduate student in 
the Iran University of Science and Technology, Like me! So we are a study couple. And we manage our small business -ElectroSadaf- that we offer electronic
security systems to people, such as CCTVs, Alarm Systems ,... .

Music is one of my hobbies. I can play Tar, Setar and Oud (Persian musical instruments) and I learnt them from famous Iranian artists such as 
Mohammad Reza Lotfi, Masoud Shaari, Arshad Tahmasebi and Mohammad Reza Ebrahimi. Todays I record some music with my friends in our small music studio every week and
share them in our instagram and youtube. It's fun and I love it.
