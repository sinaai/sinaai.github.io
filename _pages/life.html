--- layout: archive title: "Life and Hobbies" permalink: /life/ author_profile: true --- Music is one of my hobbies. I can play Tar, Setar and Oud (Persian musical instruments) and I learnt them from famous Iranian artists such as Mohammad Reza Lotfi, Masoud Shaari, Arshad Tahmasebi and Mohammad Reza Ebrahimi. Todays I record some music with my friends in our small music studio every week.
