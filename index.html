I am currently a PhD candidate in Computer Science at Portland State University, where I specialize in multilingual language models and cross-lingual fairness in Natural Language Processing. I hold a Master’s degree in Computer Engineering (Artificial Intelligence) from the Iran University of Science and Technology, where my research focused on machine learning applications to predict COVID-19 patient outcomes, including mortality rates and hospitalization durations. Additionally, I earned a Master’s in Technology Management from the University of Tehran, as well as a Bachelor’s in Industrial Engineering. My research experience spans a range of advanced AI topics, including equitable clustering algorithms and multilingual question-answering systems. Most recently, I have been conducting research in the PortNLP Lab under the guidance of Prof. Ameeta Agrawal, where I am dedicated to developing AI models that uphold fairness and accessibility across languages and cultural contexts.
