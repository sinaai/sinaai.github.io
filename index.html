About me

Research Engineer focused on transforming large language models into verifiable reasoning systems. Designed neurosymbolic frameworks (NSAR, SymCode) that convert natural language problems into executable symbolic programs, enabling deterministic verification and iterative self-correction. Experience spans mathematical reasoning, multi-target inference, multilingual long-context modeling (up to 512K tokens), hardware-aware LLM optimization, fairness-aware ML systems, and polynomial-time approximation algorithms. Emphasis on robustness, efficiency, and interpretable AI.
