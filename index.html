About Me

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Portland State University, building on a diverse academic foundation that includes two master's degrees: one in Computer Engineering from Iran University of Science and Technology and another in Technology Management from the University of Tehran. My undergraduate studies were in Industrial Engineering.

Passionate about artificial intelligence and technological innovation, I bring hands-on experience in software engineering alongside a strong focus on strategic technology development. My research interests span Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence, and I am currently engaged in advancing equitable and multilingual AI systems.
