I am currently pursuing my PhD in Computer Science at Portland State University, specializing in multilingual language models and cross-lingual fairness. Previously, I completed a Master’s in Computer Engineering (Artificial Intelligence) at Iran University of Science and Technology, where I focused on using machine learning methods to predict COVID-19 patient outcomes, including mortality rates and hospitalization lengths. My academic journey also includes a Master’s in Technology Management from the University of Tehran and a Bachelor's in Industrial Engineering from Karaj Azad University. My research experience spans diverse NLP topics, from equitable clustering algorithms to multilingual question-answering systems, with my recent work at PortNLP Lab under Prof. Ameeta Agrawal. I am driven by a passion for developing AI models that perform fairly across languages and cultural contexts.
